Subsurface and consulting background

Career experience includes geophysics and consulting roles with Halliburton Consulting, Tharwa Petroleum, PetroBel, Terracon Geotechnique with Suncor Energy, and industry-linked research with ARC Resources in Canada. Technical work spans seismic interpretation, structural framework modeling, depth conversion, seismic facies, AVO/DHI, attributes, fractures, and integrated subsurface studies.

Digital transformation focus

My current work converts seismic, well, production, map, grid, and reservoir data into reusable Python tools, machine learning workflows, dashboards, web viewers, and controlled AI-assisted workflows. The goal is practical decision support, not technology for its own sake.

Project themes

  • Blind-well validation and model QC workflows.
  • ZMAP grid processing, GeoTIFF handling, and geoscience data utilities.
  • STOIIP estimation, uncertainty, and 3D visualization concepts.
  • Fault profile analysis and interpretation QC automation.
