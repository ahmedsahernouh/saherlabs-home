FieldViewer

Digital field review for maps, wells, timelines, production, and AI-assisted technical context

FieldViewer is a web-based operations and subsurface review concept. It connects field maps, production history, well testing, completion context, status dashboards, and controlled AI Lab workflows into a single browser experience.
